# Methodology (方法论)

## English

### What is the Serenity Framework?

The Serenity Framework is a **supply-chain bottleneck investment methodology** developed by Serenity (@aleabitoreddit), a semiconductor day trader.

### Core Philosophy

> "The bottleneck is not demand, it's supply chain."

Instead of predicting demand (which is hard), focus on supply constraints (which are observable and measurable).

### Why Supply Chain Bottlenecks?

1. **Demand is predictable** (Hyperscaler CapEx guidance, government policies)
2. **Supply is constrained** (wafer fabs take 18-24 months to build)
3. **Pricing power shifts** (when supply can't meet demand, suppliers can raise prices)

### Framework Development

This framework was extracted from **839 tweets** (2025-12-26 to 2026-06-24) posted by Serenity on X.com. The analysis was done WITHOUT relying on third-party interpretations.

### How to Use This Framework

1. **Study the 5-layer framework** (see [framework/](framework/))
2. **Practice with case studies** (see [examples/](examples/))
3. **Apply to your own analysis** (use the [template](examples/template.md))
4. **Track your results** (maintain a trading journal)

### Limitations

- ⚠️ **High risk**: Serenity has had 50% drawdowns
- ⚠️ **Time intensive**: Requires tracking supply chain news daily
- ⚠️ **Semiconductor focus**: May not apply to other sectors

### Adaptation

Serenity claims this framework is **universal** (works for US stocks, China A-shares, crypto, commodities). However, adaptation may be needed for different markets.

## 中文

### 什么是 Serenity 框架？

Serenity 框架是一个**供应链瓶颈投资方法论**，由半导体日内交易员 Serenity (@aleabitoreddit) 开发。

### 核心理念

> "瓶颈不是需求，而是供应链。"

与其预测需求（这很难），不如关注供应约束（这是可观察和可测量的）。

### 为什么关注供应链瓶颈？

1. **需求是可预测的**（云计算巨头资本支出指引、政府政策）
2. **供应是受限的**（晶圆厂建设需要 18-24 个月）
3. **定价权转移**（当供应无法满足需求时，供应商可以提价）

### 框架开发

这个框架是从 Serenity 在 X.com 上发布的 **839 条推文**（2025-12-26 至 2026-06-24）中提取的。分析是**不依赖第三方解读**完成的。

### 如何使用这个框架

1. **学习五层框架**（见 [framework/](framework/)）
2. **用案例研究练习**（见 [examples/](examples/)）
3. **应用到你自己的分析**（使用[模板](examples/template.md)）
4. **跟踪你的结果**（保持交易日志）

### 局限性

- ⚠️ **高风险**：Serenity 曾经有 50% 的回撤
- ⚠️ **耗时**：需要每天跟踪供应链新闻
- ⚠️ **聚焦半导体**：可能不适用于其他行业

### 适应性

Serenity 声称这个框架是**通用的**（适用于美股、中国A股、加密货币、大宗商品）。但是，不同市场可能需要调整。
