# $AAOI Case Study (AAOI 案例分析)

## English

### Background
$AAOI (Applied Optoelectronics) - US-based optical transceiver company

### Investment Timeline

#### Initial Position (March 2026)

**Serenity's Tweet (2026-03-15):**
> "Initiating position in $AAOI at $28. They're the only US-based optical transceiver company that can benefit from CHIPS Act sourcing mandates."

**Analysis Logic:**
1. **Policy driver**: CHIPS Act requires US government/military to procure **US-made** semiconductor products
2. **Supply chain bottleneck**: Optical transceivers are critical for data centers, US domestic capacity is scarce
3. **Valuation reasonable**: $AAOI market cap ~$500M, far below peers ($COHR ~$15B)

#### Adding to Position (April 2026)

**Serenity's Tweet (2026-04-20):**
> "Adding to $AAOI at $45. Just got confirmation they're in sampling phase with a Top-3 Hyperscaler."

**Reason for Adding:**
- **Fundamental validation**: Entered sampling phase (Design Win → Sampling → Production)
- **Customer quality**: Top-3 Hyperscaler (possibly Google/Meta/Microsoft)

#### Price Target Raised (May 2026)

**Serenity's Tweet (2026-05-10):**
> "Raising price target for $AAOI to $120. If they win 2+ hyperscaler contracts, revenue could 3x in 18 months."

**Valuation Logic:**
```
Price Target = Current Revenue × 3 × Industry Average P/S Ratio
              = $200M × 3 × 2.5x P/S
              = $1.5B Market Cap / 16.8M Shares
              = ~$90/share (conservative)

Raised to $120 (assuming margin improvement)
```

#### Trimming Position (June 2026)

**Serenity's Tweet (2026-06-15):**
> "Trimming $AAOI from 8% to 3% position. Up 357% from cost. Taking profits, but keeping core position for potential hyperscaler win."

**Reason for Trimming:**
- **Price reached target** ($195 high vs $120 target)
- **Lock in profits**: Recover cost + partial profits
- **Keep core position**: In case of future contract wins

### Key Lessons

1. **Policy can create bottlenecks** (CHIPS Act sourcing mandates)
2. **Sampling phase is a key validation point** (de-risks the investment)
3. **Take profits when price reaches target** (don't get greedy)
4. **Keep core position for potential upside** (leave some on the table)

## 中文

### 背景
$AAOI（Applied Optoelectronics）- 美国光模块公司

### 投资时间线

#### 初始建仓（2026年3月）

**Serenity 的推文（2026-03-15）：**
> "在 $28 建仓 $AAOI。他们是唯一能受益于 CHIPS Act 采购 mandate 的美国光模块公司。"

**分析逻辑：**
1. **政策驱动**：CHIPS Act 要求美国政府/军方采购**美国制造**的半导体产品
2. **供应链瓶颈**：光模块是数据中心的关键组件，美国本土产能稀缺
3. **估值合理**：$AAOI 当时市值 ~$500M，远低于同行（$COHR ~$15B）

#### 加仓（2026年4月）

**Serenity 的推文（2026-04-20）：**
> "在 $45 加仓 $AAOI。刚确认他们正在与 Top-3 云计算巨头进行样品验证。"

**加仓理由：**
- **基本面验证**：进入样品验证阶段（Design Win → Sampling → Production）
- **客户质量**：Top-3 云计算巨头（可能是 Google/Meta/Microsoft）

#### 目标价上调（2026年5月）

**Serenity 的推文（2026-05-10）：**
> "上调 $AAOI 目标价至 $120。如果他们赢得 2+ 云计算巨头的合同，营收可能在 18 个月内增长 3 倍。"

**估值逻辑：**
```
目标价 = 当前营收 × 3 × 行业平均 P/S 比率
       = $200M × 3 × 2.5x P/S
       = $15亿 市值 / 1680万股
       = ~$90/股（保守）

```

**Serenity 的推文（2026-06-15）：**

- **锁定利润**：回收成本 + 部分利润
- **保留核心仓位**：以防未来赢得更多合同

### 关键教训

1. **政策可以创造瓶颈**（CHIPS Act 采购 mandate）
2. **样品验证阶段是关键验证点**（降低投资风险）
3. **当价格达到目标价时获利了结**（不要贪婪）
4. **保留核心仓位以防潜在上涨**（留一些在桌面上）
