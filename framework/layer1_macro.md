# Layer 1: Macro Capex Guidance (宏观 Capex 指引)

## English

### Objective
Determine the **demand ceiling** by analyzing hyperscaler CapEx guidance and government policies.

### Key Indicators

1. **Hyperscaler CapEx** (Magnificent 7)
   - Microsoft ($MSFT)
   - Oracle ($ORCL)
   - Google ($GOOG)
   - Amazon ($AMZN)
   - Meta ($META)
   - Apple ($AAPL)
   - NVIDIA ($NVDA)

2. **Government Policies**
   - US CHIPS Act
   - China semiconductor policies
   - EU Chips Act

3. **AI Compute Demand**
   - OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind compute procurement
   - Cloud service revenue growth

### Serenity's Words

> "$MSFT, $ORCL, $GOOG combined Capex = $225B in 2026. That's the ceiling of demand. Now work backwards to supply chain."

### Analysis Steps

1. **Collect CapEx data** from latest quarterly earnings reports
2. **Calculate total CapEx** for next 12 months
3. **Identify growth rate** (YoY)
4. **Map to supply chain** (which segments get the money?)

### Investment Decision

- **CapEx increasing** → Overweight upstream supply chain (wafers, equipment, materials)
- **CapEx decreasing** → Underweight or shift to downstream segments
- **CapEx stable** → Focus on market share winners

## 中文

### 目标
通过分析云计算巨头的资本支出指引和政府政策，确定**需求上限**。

### 关键指标

1. **云计算巨头资本支出**（Magnificent 7）
   - 微软 ($MSFT)
   - 甲骨文 ($ORCL)
   - 谷歌 ($GOOG)
   - 亚马逊 ($AMZN)
   - Meta ($META)
   - 苹果 ($AAPL)
   - 英伟达 ($NVDA)

2. **政府政策**
   - 美国 CHIPS Act
   - 中国半导体政策
   - 欧盟芯片法案

3. **AI 算力需求**
   - OpenAI、Anthropic、Google DeepMind 算力采购
   - 云服务营收增长

### Serenity 的原话

> "微软、甲骨文、谷歌 2026年合计 Capex = 2250亿美元。这是需求的上限。现在倒推到供应链。"

### 分析步骤

1. **收集资本支出数据**（从最新季度财报）
2. **计算未来 12 个月总资本支出**
3. **识别增长率**（同比）
4. **映射到供应链**（哪些环节获得资金？）

### 投资决策

- **资本支出增加** → 超配上游供应链（晶圆、设备、材料）
- **资本支出减少** → 减配或转向下游环节
- **资本支出稳定** → 关注市场份额赢家
