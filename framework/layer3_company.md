# Layer 3: Company Screening (公司筛选)

## English

### Objective
Screen companies that can **capture margin** from the bottleneck.

### Screening Criteria

| Criterion | Description | Serenity's Words |
|-----------|-------------|------------------|
| **Technical Moat** | Patents/IP protection? | "If it can be copied in 12 months, it's not an investment." |
| **Capacity Binding** | Long-term contracts with customers? | "I want to see supply agreements, not just design wins." |
| **Margin Trend** | Is margin expanding? | "Margin expansion is the proof that bottleneck thesis is working." |
| **Management Execution** | Can they deliver on time? | "Too many optical companies overpromise and underdeliver." |
| **Valuation** | Reflected in next 12 months earnings? | "I'll pay 50x P/E if I can see 100% EPS growth." |

### Screening Process

1. **Start with bottleneck segment** (from Layer 2)
2. **List all public companies** in this segment
3. **Apply screening criteria** (technical moat, capacity binding, etc.)
4. **Rank by conviction** (Tier 1 / Tier 2 / Speculative)
5. **Validate with supply chain intelligence** (OSINT, earnings calls, supplier audits)

### Red Flags

- ❌ No long-term supply agreements
- ❌ Management overpromises (missed deadlines)
- ❌ Margin compression (despite bottleneck)
- ❌ High customer concentration (one customer = high risk)

## 中文

### 目标
筛选能够从瓶颈中**捕获利润**的公司。

### 筛选标准

| 标准 | 说明 | Serenity 的原话 |
|------|------|----------------|
| **技术壁垒** | 是否有专利/IP 保护？ | "如果能在 12 个月内复制，那就不是投资。" |
| **产能绑定** | 是否与客户签订长期合同？ | "我想看到供应协议，而不仅仅是设计胜利。" |
| **毛利率趋势** | 毛利率是否在扩张？ | "毛利率扩张是瓶颈 thesis 有效的证明。" |
| **管理层执行力** | 是否能按时交付？ | "太多光学公司过度承诺、交付不足。" |

### 筛选流程

1. **从瓶颈环节开始**（从第二层）
2. **列出该环节所有上市公司**
3. **应用筛选标准**（技术壁垒、产能绑定等）
4. **按确信度排名**（Tier 1 / Tier 2 / 投机）
5. **用供应链情报验证**（OSINT、财报电话会议、供应商审计）

### 危险信号

- ❌ 没有长期供应协议
- ❌ 管理层过度承诺（错过截止日期）
- ❌ 毛利率压缩（尽管有瓶颈）
- ❌ 客户集中度过高（一个客户 = 高风险）
