# Layer 4: Position Sizing (仓位管理)

## English

### Objective
Determine **how much to allocate** to each position based on conviction level.

### Serenity's Words

> "Position sizing is more art than science. I use a tiered approach: Core, Swing, and Lottery."

### Position Sizing Framework

| Position Type | Allocation | Holding Period | Stop Loss | Example |
|---------------|-------------|----------------|----------|---------|
| **Core** | 5-15% | 6-18 months | -30% | $MU, $MRVL |
| **Swing** | 2-5% | 1-6 months | -20% | $AAOI, $SIVE |
| **Lottery** | <1% | <1 month | -50% | $WYFI, $GRRR |

### Average Up (追涨) Rules

- ✅ **Only for Core positions**
- ✅ Stock up 20% → Add 50% more if thesis intact
- ✅ "I average up, not down. If it's going up, the market is validating my thesis."

### Trim (减仓) Rules

- Stock up 100% → Trim 30% (lock in profits)
- Stock up 200% → Trim 50% (recover cost)
- Thesis broken → Exit immediately (no hesitation)

### Stop Loss (止损) Rules

| Position Type | Stop Loss | Reasoning |
|---------------|-----------|-----------|
| **Core** | -30% | Give more room for high-conviction plays |
| **Swing** | -20% | Strict execution |
| **Lottery** | -50% | Accept high volatility |

### Serenity's Words

> "I don't set price targets. I set thesis validation checkpoints. If the bottleneck thesis is intact, I hold. If not, I exit."

### Position Sizing Process

1. **Assign conviction level** (Core / Swing / Lottery)
2. **Determine initial allocation** (based on framework above)
3. **Set thesis validation checkpoints** (what would prove/disprove the thesis?)
4. **Plan exit strategy** (both profit-taking and stop-loss)
5. **Review and adjust** (weekly or after major news)

## 中文

### 目标
根据确信度水平确定**每个仓位分配多少**。

### Serenity 的原话

> "仓位管理是艺术多于科学。我用分层方法：核心、摆动、彩票。"

### 仓位管理框架

| 仓位类型 | 仓位大小 | 持仓时间 | 止损 | 示例 |
|----------|----------|----------|------|------|
| **核心** | 5-15% | 6-18 个月 | -30% | $MU, $MRVL |
| **摆动** | 2-5% | 1-6 个月 | -20% | $AAOI, $SIVE |
| **彩票** | <1% | <1 个月 | -50% | $WYFI, $GRRR |

### Average Up（追涨）规则

- ✅ **仅适用于核心仓位**
- ✅ 股价上涨 20% → 如果 thesis 完好，加仓 50%
- ✅ "我追涨，不追跌。如果股价在涨，说明市场在验证我的 thesis。"

### Trim（减仓）规则

- 股价上涨 100% → 减仓 30%（锁定利润）
- 股价上涨 200% → 减仓 50%（回收成本）
- Thesis 破坏 → 立即退出（不犹豫）

### Stop Loss（止损）规则

| 仓位类型 | 止损 | 理由 |
|----------|------|------|
| **核心** | -30% | 给高确信度交易更大的容忍空间 |
| **摆动** | -20% | 严格执行 |
| **彩票** | -50% | 接受高波动性 |

### Serenity 的原话

> "我不设价格目标。我设 thesis 验证检查点。如果瓶颈 thesis 完好，我持有。如果不完好，我退出。"

### 仓位管理流程

1. **分配确信度水平**（核心 / 摆动 / 彩票）
2. **确定初始仓位**（基于上述框架）
3. **设置 thesis 验证检查点**（什么会证明/证伪 thesis？）
4. **计划退出策略**（包括获利了结和止损）
5. **审查和调整**（每周或重大新闻后）
